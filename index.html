About Full Circle Psychology

Boutique psychology practice dedicated to excellence

Full Circle Psychology is a boutique psychology practice dedicated to providing comprehensive, evidence-based psychological services. Our team of experienced psychologists specializes in assessments and therapy for children, adolescents, and adults.

We pride ourselves on short wait times, personalized care, and building strong therapeutic relationships. Our practice serves clients both in-person in Brooklyn, NY, and via telehealth across 42 states.

Our commitment to excellence ensures that every client receives the highest quality psychological care tailored to their unique needs and circumstances.
